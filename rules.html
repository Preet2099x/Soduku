Sudoku Rules

Sudoku is a mind-numbing number puzzle game. The rules are as simple as your intellect:

  1. Fill the grid so that each row, each column, and each of the nine 3Ã—3 subgrids contain all of the numbers from 1 to 9.

  2. Use the "Check" button to verify your progress. You can only use "Check" 3 times

  3. Your goal is to complete the puzzle as quickly as possible with the highest score. The score is calculated based on the number of correct cells filled..

  4. The game has a time limit of 5 minutes. If the time runs out, the puzzle will be automatically submitted. Not that anyone cares about your failure anyway.

Enjoy playing Sudoku! Or try not to embarrass yourself too much.
